Server Rules

General
  • Be respectful to all players
  • No hate speech or discrimination
  • No spamming or excessive caps
  • Follow all staff instructions
  • No exploits or cheats
  • English only in global chat
  • No impersonation of staff
  • No threats or harassment
  • No advertising other servers
  • Use common sense
Building
  • No griefing others' builds
  • No stealing resources
  • Claim your land responsibly
  • No lava casting or destructive machines
  • Respect community areas
  • No blocking spawn points
  • Ask before editing others' builds
  • Rollback abuse may result in ban
  • Keep builds within server guidelines
  • Build responsibly and creatively
PvP
  • No kill aura or auto attacks
  • No spawn killing
  • No combat logging
  • Engage in fair fights only
  • No traps that break rules
  • No teleport abuse in fights
  • No boosting with alt accounts
  • No PvP glitches or exploits
  • PvP zones only where allowed
  • Respect other players during PvP
Discord
  • Follow Discord Terms of Service
  • No NSFW content in channels
  • No mic spam or loud noises
  • No false reports to staff
  • No DM advertising
  • Use the correct channels for topics
  • No impersonating staff or others
  • No raids or disruptive behavior
  • No unnecessary drama or fights
  • Be mature and respectful
